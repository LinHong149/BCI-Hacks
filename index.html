About

BCI is a hackathon that will last for two weeks hosted by the Bethune Computer Science Club, for the students of Bethune! All grades from Bethune are welcome and we will provide workshops to get you started. Create your dream project with a team or solo!

BCI Hacks is hosted by the Executives of the Bethune Computer Science Club

Schedule

Nov.29 - Start of hackathon and workshops

The hackathon starts with an opening ceremony where we will introduce you to BCI Hacks and announce the theme. We will also hold introduction workshops for web development and Arduino.

Dec.6 - Mid-hackathon workshops and mentor sessions

We will be having indepth web development and arduino workshops. We will also be having mentor sessions to help you with your projects one on one.

Dec.10 - End of hackathon

The submission forms will be closed and all projects have to be submitted before hand.

Dec.13 - Winners ceremony

All projects will be judged and the winner will be announced!

Why Participate?

Want to challenge yourself?

Hackathons are a great place to challenge yourself to create something within a certain period of time.

Want to learn more about computer science?

We will be hosting live workshops to help you with your project along the way.

Want to be rewarded for your hard work?

Prizes will be given to winners of each category.
